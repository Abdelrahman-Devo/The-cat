Food

Cat food is food intended for consumption by cats. As with all animals, cats have specific nutritional requirements that must be added to their foods. There are certain nutrients; Like many vitamins, amino acids are degraded by heat, pressure, and chemical treatments used during the production process, and therefore they must be added after the production process to avoid nutritional deficiency, which may lead to many complications. For example, taurine acid, which is present in meat, decomposes during the production process, so synthetic taurine acid is usually added after the production process, as any long-term deficiency of this acid may lead to retinal degeneration, loss of vision, and cardiac arrest.
